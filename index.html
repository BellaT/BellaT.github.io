Shindig

Shindig is an app created for music lovers everywhere, where they can share the music they love with their friends, and invite them to contribute to the party. Shindig enables a user to make a playlist using YouTube videos and invite friends to add to it from anywhere. All they have to do is log in to the app and add a track and it will be automatically added to the playlist queue (implemented by using sockets). This way everyone can be the DJ.
